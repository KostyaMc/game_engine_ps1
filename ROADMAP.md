# Roadmap

## Stage 1: Math Foundation

- Vec2, Vec3, Vec4.
- Mat4.
- Translation, rotation, scale.
- Camera transform.
- Perspective projection.
- Model, world, view, clip, NDC, and screen spaces.

## Stage 2: Software Renderer

- Framebuffer.
- Line rendering.
- Wireframe rendering.
- Triangle rasterization.
- Backface culling.
- Z-buffer.
- Perspective projection.

## Stage 3: PS1-Style Constraints

- Affine texture mapping.
- Vertex lighting.
- Low internal resolution.
- Fixed-point style precision loss.
- Vertex jitter.
- No subpixel precision.
- Limited color depth.
- Color banding.
- Dithering.
- Texture size limits.

## Stage 4: Engine Architecture

- Renderer.
- Scene.
- Entity.
- Transform.
- Mesh.
- Texture.
- Material.
- Camera.

## Stage 5: Model Formats

- OBJ loading.
- A simple custom model format.

## Stage 6: Mini Game

- Technical demo first.
- Then a small PS1-style exploration or survival horror prototype.
